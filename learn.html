Start Small It might not sound like a big change but if each person does it, it makes a large impact.
ReUse Purchase items that are meant to be used over and over instead of using something non-recyclable once.
Recycle Get recycling bins and stop throwing away cans, bottles, and cardboard boxes.
Repurpose Use materials for something other than originally intended; save money & throw less away.
